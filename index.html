About Me

Clinical expertise, powered by data

I'm Bimala Joshi — a clinical data analyst and BI professional with a rare combination of hands-on hospital operations experience and graduate-level data science training. For over a decade I managed outpatient, inpatient, ICU, OT and emergency services, tracking KPIs like ICU utilization, bed occupancy, patient satisfaction and ALOS — metrics that directly inform clinical and operational decisions.

I bring that clinical fluency to every analysis. Whether it's designing a real-time clinical dashboard, writing complex SQL for reporting, or surfacing trends in patient outcomes, I connect the technical and the clinical to drive measurable impact. My analytical skills transfer across industries, and I'm open to opportunities in any sector where data drives decisions. Published researcher. HIPAA certified. Presidential award recipient.

Energy Consumption Forecasting for Smart Grid

Tackled forecasting inefficiency for smart grid operators by building predictive models on 187,728 hourly records spanning 2001–2022, with 34 internal (load, PV, fuel cell) and external (weather) variables. After KNN imputation and temporal feature engineering, evaluated Linear Regression, Ridge, HistGradientBoosting, XGBoost, LightGBM, and ANN.
